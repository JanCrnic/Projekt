Southampton closing in on Mislav Orsic signing

Southampton are close to signing Dinamo Zagreb's Mislav Orsic.

It is understood that a deal has been agreed that will see the 30-year-old move to St Mary's in the January transfer window. Southampton are keen to bring in their targets quickly and Nathan Jones wanted to add experience to his side.

The south coast side are believed to have offered around 6 million pounds plus add-ons for Orsic and the improved offer has seen Zagreb accept the bid.

Everton and Nottingham Forest have also been linked with a move for Orsic, but it appears the Croatia international looks set to join Nathan Jones' team.

Orsic represented Croatia at the 2022 World Cup in Qatar and scored a goal during his nation's third-place play-off victory over Morocco.
